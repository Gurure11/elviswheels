About Us

Elvis Wheels, based in Vanderbijlpark, has been offering excellent and reliable tyre and rim services for over ten years. With two well-established branches, we specialize in wheel balancing, aluminium welding, suspension work, brake pad replacement, shock replacement, new and used tyres supply, rim repairs, rim polishing, PCD hole changes, and diamond cutting. Our commitment to quality, safety, and customer satisfaction sets us apart in the industry.
